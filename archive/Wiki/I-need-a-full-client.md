**Note: The ClientDownloader does not download any content for the Data folder! FOLLOW NEXT STEP!**

To get the rest data you may use [WowRegeneration](https://github.com/The-Cataclysm-Preservation-Project/wowregeneration).
Because of legal reasons there will be no torrent or hosting of a full client. Use the provided tools to retrieve the original data from Retail servers.
